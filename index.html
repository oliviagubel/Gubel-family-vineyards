How It All Began

A Family's Journey

It was Dr. Isaac Gubel's love for the early Cabernets from the Mendoza region of Argentina that awakened his young son's taste for the novel grape. Gustavo would sneak sips of wine at family barbecues on "The Island" in the outskirts of Buenos Aires.

94 Points

The Tasting Panel / Somm

A single vineyard Cab grown on volcanic soils, aged 20 months in French oak. Fresh aromas of blackberry and vanilla lead to teeth-coating tannins. Sun-drenched boysenberry displays regal presence. The wine is refined and linear, with intensity of flavor.
